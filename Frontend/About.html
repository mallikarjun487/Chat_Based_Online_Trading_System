About TradeBridge

Your gateway to simple online trading

TradeBridge is a chat-based online trading platform built to help users manage their investments, track portfolios, and stay informed with live market data and financial news.

What We Offer

  • User accounts — Secure login and registration with portfolio tracking.
  • Buy, Sell & Hold — Place orders and maintain a full transaction history.
  • Financial chatbot — Get stock prices, portfolio summaries, and market news via chat.
  • Live news feed — Latest financial headlines from trusted APIs.
  • Admin tools — User management for platform administrators.

Our Mission

We aim to make investing more accessible with a clean, easy-to-use interface and tools that combine trading, portfolio management, and market information in one place.

TradeBridge is intended for educational and prototype use. Always do your own research and consult a licensed advisor before making investment decisions.
